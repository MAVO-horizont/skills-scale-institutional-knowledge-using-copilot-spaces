# OctoAcme — Roles and Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project management to ensure clarity, accountability, and effective collaboration.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers are responsible for translating business and user needs into intuitive, effective designs. They collaborate closely with Product Managers and Developers.

### Responsibilities
- Create user flows, wireframes, and visual designs
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Deliver design assets for development
- Ensure accessible and responsive design compliance

### Goals
- Optimize user experience by meeting customer needs
- Ensure designs contribute to measurable business outcomes
- Support iterative design and feedback processes

### Communication
- Design reviews with team members
- Coordination with Product Managers on requirements
- Handoff and feedback with developers during implementation

---

## DevOps/Platform Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers maintain the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and environments to ensure deployments are reliable, secure, and scalable.

### Responsibilities
- Manage build/release pipelines and automation
- Own environment configuration and monitoring
- Collaborate on incident response and operational improvements
- Enable rapid, safe releases

### Goals
- Reduce deployment and infrastructure-related bottlenecks
- Support a stable, scalable development environment
- Improve team velocity through automation

### Communication
- Collaborate with developers to resolve build or deployment issues
- Coordinate releases with Project Managers
- Escalate environmental risks to stakeholders when required

---

## Documentation Specialists / Technical Writers

### Role Summary
Documentation Specialists produce and maintain clear, accessible internal and external documentation that supports development and user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Author product, API, and process documentation
- Edit and structure release notes and onboarding materials
- Maintain up-to-date project knowledge bases
- Facilitate documentation reviews

### Goals
- Ensure documentation clarifies workflows and reduces onboarding friction
- Improve end-user satisfaction with product communication
- Support continuity of institutional knowledge

### Communication
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers for technical details
- Work with QA and Support teams for accuracy and clarity

---

## Customer/Support Liaisons

### Role Summary
Customer/Support Liaisons connect end users and the development team, helping gather feedback while triaging issues promptly.

### Responsibilities
- Gather user feedback and feature requests
- Monitor support tickets relevant to project priorities
- Clarify recurring issues and track their root cause
- Relay high-impact customer risks to engineering and PMs

### Goals
- Reduce customer pain points by surfacing key trends
- Proactively identify and escalate user-critical risks
- Improve alignment with user stories and outcomes

### Communication
- Weekly meetings with support teams
- Ongoing updates to Product Managers for requirement grooming
- Participation in triage sessions for priority management
